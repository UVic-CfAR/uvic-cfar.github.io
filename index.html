Mission Statement

Innovation is a cornerstone in the development of market turn-key solutions for the UAV market for civil applications. The Center for Aerospace Research aims to spur innovation, investment, and attention to the positive role this technology can play in betterment of society.

We provide turn-key research services, design, fabrication, ground/flight testing and operations for potential applications, at a reduced risk for the industry partner, while furthering the technological state of the art and transfer in this new sector of the aerospace industry.
